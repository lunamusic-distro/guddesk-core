Customer support tools have a problem. They're either expensive, locked-in, or both. When we needed a modern messaging platform for our own projects, we kept running into the same wall: pay $50+ per seat per month for features that should be table stakes, or duct-tape together a dozen open-source tools that don't talk to each other.

So we built GudDesk.

## What is GudDesk?

GudDesk is a complete customer messaging platform — live chat, shared inbox, knowledge base, AI agents, and workflow automations — all bundled into a single open-source project licensed under GPL-3.0.

Think of it as the Intercom experience without the Intercom price tag.

## Why We Built It

Three things frustrated us about the current landscape:

1. **Per-seat pricing is broken.** Your entire team should be able to jump into a conversation without your CFO having a panic attack.
2. **AI should be a first-class citizen.** Not an upsell. Not an add-on. Baked in from day one.
3. **You should own your data.** Full stop. Self-host it, export it, move it wherever you want.

GudDesk addresses all three.

## What's Included

Out of the box, you get:

- **Live Chat Widget** — A lightweight, embeddable widget you can drop into any site with two lines of code.
- **Shared Inbox** — Email, chat, and bot conversations unified in a single view for your whole team.
- **Knowledge Base** — Write help articles, organize them into collections, and let your AI agents suggest them automatically.
- **AI Agents** — Three built-in agents (GudBot, TriageBot, OnboardBot) that actually resolve tickets — not just parrot canned replies.
- **Automations** — Route, tag, assign, and escalate without lifting a finger.
- **Plugin API** — Build custom integrations or install community plugins from the marketplace.

## Free for Small Teams

GudDesk is free to use for teams of up to 3 agents. No credit card required, no feature gates on the free tier, no time-limited trials. If you're a startup or a small team, you can run your entire support operation on GudDesk without paying a cent.

For larger teams, our Pro plan adds advanced AI features, priority support, and custom branding removal.

## Self-Hostable

Every line of GudDesk is open source. Clone the repo, run `docker compose up`, and you've got a fully functional instance running on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your servers.

## What's Next

We're just getting started. Here's what's on the roadmap:

- **Slack and Teams integrations** — Reply to customers from where your team already works.
- **Custom agent builder** — A visual editor for creating AI agent workflows.
- **Mobile app** — Native iOS and Android apps for on-the-go support.
- **Multilingual knowledge base** — Auto-translate articles for global teams.
