The email integration lets you manage support emails alongside chat conversations in your GudDesk shared inbox.

## How It Works

1. Forward incoming emails to your GudDesk inbox address
2. Emails appear as conversations in your shared inbox
3. Reply from GudDesk — your response is sent as a regular email
4. The full email thread is preserved in the conversation

## Setting Up Email Forwarding

### 1. Find Your Inbox Address

Each GudDesk workspace has a unique inbox email address:

```
support@inbox.guddesk.com
```

Find yours in **Dashboard > Settings > Email**.

### 2. Configure Forwarding

Set up email forwarding from your support email to your GudDesk inbox address:

**Gmail:**
1. Go to Settings > Forwarding and POP/IMAP
2. Add your GudDesk inbox address as a forwarding address
3. Confirm the forwarding request

**Custom domain:**
Set up a mail forwarding rule or MX record to forward `support@yourdomain.com` to your GudDesk inbox address.

### 3. Configure Reply-From Address

To send replies from your own domain (e.g., `support@yourdomain.com`):

1. Go to **Settings > Email > Reply-From**
2. Enter your custom email address
3. Verify domain ownership via DNS (TXT record)
4. GudDesk sends replies on your behalf via Resend

## Email Threading

GudDesk preserves email threads correctly:

- Replies include proper `In-Reply-To` and `References` headers
- Customers see a normal email thread in their inbox
- CC/BCC recipients are tracked in the conversation metadata

## Attachments

Email attachments are automatically:

- Displayed inline in the conversation view
- Stored securely and linked from the conversation
- Scannable by your AI agents for context

## Auto-Responder

Set up an automatic acknowledgment email:

1. Go to **Settings > Email > Auto-Responder**
2. Enable and customize the message
3. Optionally include links to your knowledge base

Example:

> Thanks for reaching out! We've received your message and will get back to you within 24 hours. In the meantime, you might find an answer in our [Help Center](https://your-domain.com/help).

## Self-Hosted Email

For self-hosted instances, configure these environment variables:

```env
RESEND_API_KEY="your-resend-api-key"
```

GudDesk uses [Resend](https://resend.com) for email delivery. You can use the free tier (100 emails/day) for small teams or upgrade for higher volume.
